The child of Henri Brabant and Maria von Hohenstaufen, was born in Brabant, Meuse, Lorraine, France in 1233. He was orphaned at the age of 15 following the death of his mother in 1235 and father in 1248.

He died on Feb 21, 1261 in Marbourg, Clervaux, Diekirch, Luxembourg.
