Heiress Archarda of Carolingian (née Bar-Sur-Aube) 925 - 970

Heiress was the 35 times great-grandmother of Stephanie Hardesty (née Teten), was born in La Ferte Sur Aube, Aube, Champagne, France in 925 and married Seigneur Charles (de) Courcy Carolingian (with whom she had 2 children: Nocher Bar sur Aube and Wigelius Duke Wigerius Charles Of Lorraine Teutonicus) ((De)) in Champagne, France around 940.

She died after 970 in Lorraine, Moselle, Lorraine, France.
