Elizabeth DEvereux Countess Sutton (née Clonard) 1170 - 1200

Elizabeth was the 26 times great-grandmother of Stephanie Hardesty (née Teten), was born in Sutton On Trent, Nottinghamshire, England in 1170 and married 5th Sutton (with whom she had 3 children: Richard (De), Rowland Sir Lord Sutton and Thomas (6th Lord) (De)) in England in 1190.

She died in Sutton On Trent in 1200.
