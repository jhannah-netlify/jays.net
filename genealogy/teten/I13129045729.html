Beatrix Barbarossa Holy Roman Emperor (née Deburgundy) 1145 - 1185

[ Home ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

Beatrix was the 27 times great-grandmother of Stephanie Hardesty (née Teten), was born in Mâcon, Ain, Rhone-Alpes, France in 1145 and married Frederick Holy (with whom she had 9 children: Beatrice, Friedrich V, Henry VI Hohenstaufens Roman Emperor of, Heinrich VI Emperor, Otto Of, Gisela von, Agnes, Konrad II and Phillip) in Würzburg, Stadt Wurzburg, Bayern, Germany on Jun 9, 1156.

She died on Nov 15, 1185 in Château, Dordogne, Aquitaine, France.
