Frederick was the 27 times great-grandfather of Stephanie Hardesty (née Teten), was born in Waiblingen, Ostalbkreis, Baden-Wuerttemberg, Germany in 1122 and married Beatrix Deburgundy (with whom he had 9 children: Beatrice, Friedrich V, Henry VI Hohenstaufens Roman Emperor of, Heinrich VI Emperor, Otto Of, Gisela von, Agnes, Konrad II and Phillip) in Würzburg, Stadt Wurzburg, Bayern, Germany on Jun 9, 1156.
