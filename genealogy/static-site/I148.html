Andrew Sappenfield 1817 - 1894

[ Home ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

The 4th of 15 children of (Teis) Matthias Sappenfield and Christina Grimes, Andrew was the 4 times great-grandfather of Jay Hannah, was born in Rowan Co., North Carolina on May 20, 1817 and married his second cousin on his mother's side Margaret Grimes (with whom he had 10 children: John M. G, Sarah Elizabeth, Eliza E, Absalom W, Maria M, William H, Alfred Marion, Andrew J, Brunett Caroline and Ephriam M) in Des Moines Co., Iowa on Nov 9, 1837.

He died on Jun 15, 1894 in Jefferson Co., Iowa1.
