She married William Brennan (with whom she had 8 children: Cecile Ellen, James Warren, Mary Elberta, John Robert, Esther Agnes, William Edward, Alice Florence and Joseph Jonathan) in Kingfisher, Kingfisher Co., OK on Jan 9, 1900

She died on Jun 10, 1973 in Bell, Los Angeles Co. Calif.
