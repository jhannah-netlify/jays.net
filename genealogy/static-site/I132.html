Albert Stark (Es) 1843 - 1919

[ Home ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

The child of William Stark and Rebecca Ragsdale, Albert was the four times great-uncle of Jay Hannah, was born in Parke Co., Indiana in 1843 and married Margaret Sears (with whom he had 4 children: William Henry Stark, Louella R Stark, Charles Elmer Stark and Carrie E Stark) in Jefferson Co., Iowa on Aug 24, 1869.

He died on Aug 20, 1919 in St Joseph, Missouri.
