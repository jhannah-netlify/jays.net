Eunice Electa Overton (née McVey) 1892 - 1982

[ Home ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

The youngest of 7 children of Marion McVey and Charity Scarborough, Eunice was the first cousin three-times-removed on the father's side of Jay Hannah, was born in Henry Co., IA on Apr 19, 1892. She was married twice - to Victor Huxley (on Oct 2, 1913 in Salem Twp., Henry Co., IA) John Overton (on Dec 27, 1947 in Mt. Pleasant, Henry Co., IA).

She died in Henry Co. in 1982.
