Mary Catherine Hickenbottom (née Hannah) 1896 - 1975

The 3rd of 6 children of John Hannah and Viola Courter, , Mary was the first cousin three-times-removed on the father's side of Jay Hannah, was born in NW of Salem on Big Cedar Riv., Henry Co., IA on Jan 3, 1896 and married Matthew Hickenbottom (with whom she had 6 children: James Warren, Roy Lyle, Ella Mae, Harlan Edward, Agnes Marie and Doris Lavonne) in Morning Sun, Hannah home, IA on Feb 20, 1916.

She died on Feb 18, 1975 in Almeda Calif. Bur. Elmwood Cmty., Morning Sun, IA.
