Eva Leota Davis (née Hamilton) 1870 - 1933

[ Home ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

The youngest of 9 children of George Hamilton and Hannah Bunker, Eva was the first cousin three-times-removed on the father's side of Jay Hannah, was born in Kennekuk, Atchison Co., KS on Dec 16, 1870 and was married twice - to Elmer Ellsworth 'Else' Vining (on May 13, 1888 in Baker, Brown Co., KS) William Davis (on Feb 19, 1913, following the death of Elmer Ellsworth "Else" on Oct 7, 1908). She had 2 children, both with Elmer Ellsworth 'Else' Vining: Hallie and Guy Ellsworth.

She died on Nov 14, 1933 in Horton, Brown Co., Kansas.
