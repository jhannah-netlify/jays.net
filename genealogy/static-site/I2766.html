Ruth Dicks (née Hiatt) 1735 - 1775

[ Home ] | [ Surnames Index ] | [ Family History ]

The child of George Hiatt and Martha Wakefield, Ruth was the six times great-aunt of Jay Hannah, was born in Loudon Co., VA in 1735. She married Zacharias Dicks in New Garden MM, Guilford Co., NC on Dec 8, 1756.

She died after 1775 in Orange Co., VA.
