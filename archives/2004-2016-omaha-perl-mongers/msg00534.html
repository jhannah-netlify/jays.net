> Actually, I wouldn't 'use Env' at all. That's just a "shortcut" for
> Perl's built-in %ENV. I'd do this:
>
> #!/usr/bin/perl -w
>
> $ENV{Var1} ||= "DefaultValue1";

> my $Var3 = "$ENV{Var1} -- $ENV{Var2}";
