All right! The Android Scripting Environment (ASE) now supports Perl!  Download an .apk of ASE here: http://code.google.com/p/android-scripting/downloads/list
I might write an app for downloading and installing CPAN modules as  soon as I buy another microSD, my old one got lost :(
Also, ASE has support as a Locale plugin (the closest thing to a  crontab system on Android) to run scripts after an event occurs (a  contact calls, it is a certain time, or you are in a certain area via  GPS)
Anyway, it seems cool, no idea how much support or how buggy it is.  Hopefully at least LWP::Simple will work
