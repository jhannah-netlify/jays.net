Brian Edwards - Overview of Dancer Perl web micro-framework.
An overview of Dancer, the minimalist Perl web framework "inspired by Sinatra".
Dancer is intuitive and straight-forward - gets things running quickly, clean, lean and powerful. From small sites to full-scale enterprise web apps Dancer does the job without getting in the way.
Flexible enough to keep Modern Perl monkeys happy and make an old procedural gorilla productive again.
