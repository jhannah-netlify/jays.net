> Well, there's the dreaded:
> $b = ($a > b$ ? $a : $b);

This expression returns the maximum of $a and $b:

    [ $a => $b ] -> [ $a <= $b ]

Though less readable than your version, it's more enjoyable to read. :-)
As much as I like it, I can't recommend it for production code.
