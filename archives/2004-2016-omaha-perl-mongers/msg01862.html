uri_for() is a sweet little chunk of magic.

I throw this into my template:
<a href="[% Catalyst.uri_for('refresh_sold_counts') %]">[refresh] </a>
And the appropriate URL relative to my current Action happens automatically... In this case: 

   http://cac-dev.omnihotels.com:3000/omnicrs/hrms/refresh_sold_counts
   (not publicly accessible)
So I can move my Controllers and/or applications around at will and all my URLs just magically work. :)
