Is this what you’re trying to say?

my $EnvVar1 = ($EnvVar1 || "DefaultValue1");

my $EnvVar2 = ($EnvVar2 || "DefaultValue2");

my $Var3 = "$EnvVar1 -- $EnvVar2";

If for some reason $EnvVar1 doesn’t have a value, use the value “DefaultValue1”
