Just switch your pager to a redirect to a file:

  DB<8> o pager=>/tmp/j.txt
               pager = '>/tmp/j.txt'
  DB<9> | x $self
Now you've got a /tmp/j.txt file containing what 'x $self' would have shown. Sweet.
