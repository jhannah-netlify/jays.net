$new_string = join("\n", split("|", $string));

Of course, it should be:

$new_string = join("\n", split(/\|/, $string));
Yeah, if you always write your split()s with the /x/ instead of "x", then it makes it easier to remember that it's ALWAYS a regex, even if it's in quotes.
