Looking at the source of Crypt/Tea.pm, it appears your possible chars are
 
   [A-Za-z0-9\-_]
 
So you could "do one or more of any of those":
 
   my ($encrypted) = ($row =~ /attr_key="([A-Za-z0-9\-_]+)"/);
 
or "one or more anything that is not a quote":
 
   my ($encrypted) = ($row =~ /attr_key="([^"]+)"/);
 
or "one or more anything, non-greedy":
 
   my ($encrypted) = ($row =~ /attr_key="(.+?)"/);
 
?  untested...
